# 📊 Financial Microstructure Modeling via Temporal Hawkes Processes

🚀 This repository explores the **endogenous reflexivity** of the Bitcoin (BTC-USD) market by applying a self-exciting temporal Hawkes process to high-frequency return data.

## 📝 Overview
The project models volatility clustering as a branching process where extreme price movements—defined as absolute hourly returns exceeding 1.5%, trigger subsequent market activity. Using **Maximum Likelihood Estimation (MLE)**, the framework quantifies the degree of market contagion and the relaxation time required for the system to return to its baseline state.

## 🧮 Mathematical Framework
The conditional intensity $\lambda(t)$, representing the instantaneous rate of events given the history $\mathcal{H}_t$, is defined as:

$$\lambda(t|\mathcal{H}_{t}) = \mu + \sum_{i:t_{i}<t} \alpha \beta e^{-\beta(t-t_{i})}$$

* **$\mu$ (Background Rate):** The frequency of exogenous, news-driven shocks.
* **$\alpha$ (Branching Ratio):** The average number of "offspring" events triggered by a single shock, representing market endogeneity.
* **$\beta$ (Decay Rate):** The speed at which the impact of a shock dissipates over time.

## 📈 Empirical Results (BTC-USD)
Analysis of hourly log-returns reveals a highly self-exciting market regime:

| Parameter | Value | Market Insight |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Branching Ratio ($\alpha$)** | **0.53266** | ~53% of volatility is internally generated by the market. |
| **Decay Rate ($\beta$)** | **0.04748** | A shock half-life of approximately **14.6 hours**. |
| **Background Rate ($\mu$)** | **0.00937** | Baseline rate of roughly 0.01 shocks per hour. |

💡 **Insight:** The results indicate that over half of extreme volatility events are driven by internal feedback loops, such as algorithmic cascades or liquidation spirals.

## ⚡ Systematic Trading Applications
The intensity signal $\lambda(t)$ provides a quantitative foundation for institutional use-cases:

* **🛡️ Dynamic Risk Management:** Scaling down position sizes or halting execution algorithms during the 14-hour relaxation window following an intensity spike to avoid liquidity cascades.
* **💰 Liquidity Provision:** Asymmetrically widening bid-ask spreads during high-intensity regimes to monetize adverse selection and capture higher liquidity premiums.
